About Me

"Hi! I’m Nathalie. Born in Bavaria and raised by my Scottish family, I’m a creative living in Berlin with a passion for UI design and English Breakfast tea. I grew up in a musical patch-work family spending most weekends with my parents and five siblings near the bavarian alps. Next to learning the piano, drawing was one of the first creative things I was very passionate about. But my journey as a designer began in my final year at High school, when I fell in love with Surrealism, and the work of artists Dali and Magritte. Fascinated by the endless possibilities of art and imagination, I was inspired to persue a creative career in fashion design, which ulmimately lead me to explore design in the tech industry.In my former role as senior fashion designer at H&M, I gained experience creating products for variety of customer groups worldwide. While utilizing trend forecasts and concepts to create collections on tight deadlines, I mentored junior designers and collaborated with project managers, print designers and production managers. After working with a global company and newly redirecting my design expertise to freelance projects, I am inspired to add a combination of creative vision, interpersonal skills and well-designed aesthetics to the field of UI design. I am currently continuing my front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) education and working on client projects in particular branding, UI and Illustration projects to keep my skills fresh and continuing to learn, grow and evolve. I look forward to improving my design abilities, helping achieve design goals and working in a supportive and creative company-cultured environment."
